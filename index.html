Research One

   Study on photocatalytic properties of ternary material NixCoySz

·Responsibilities :

   Experimental operation, material preparation, material testing, data collation, data analysis, scientific research mapping, PPT report, paper writing

·Current Stage :

   Data arrangement and paper Writing

·Partial Content Display :

Figure 1. Flow chart of experiment
    The preparation of the film electrode in this experiment mainly includes the following three steps:
  • (1)solution preparation: 1.1885 g of the drug 1, 2.379332 g of the drug 2 and 1.5224 g of the drug 3 were added to 10 ml of the solvent 4, and stirred at room temperature for half an hour to form a desired homogeneous solution, and the solution was blue.
  • (2) Film deposition: 5 ml of the resulting blue homogeneous solution was spin coated onto the FTO glass at 2000 r/min and then placed on a hot plate for drying. At the same time, the carbon paper after the carbon deposition treatment was immersed in a petri dish containing the obtained blue solution, and the soaking time was 5, 15, and 30 minutes, respectively.
  • (3)Annealing: The deposited FTO glass flakes and carbon paper were annealed at 250 ° C for 1 hour under argon to obtain the desired thin film electrode.
